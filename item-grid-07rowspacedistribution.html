Distribution

Row Content Space Distribution

Sometimes... Sometimes audience, columns just need a little space in their row.

  • grid__row--$screenSize-around
  • grid__row--$screenSize-between

NOTE: can't be used with easy columns.

Example

This row needs a little space around it's columns: grid__row--xs-around

This row needs a little space between it's columns: grid__row--xs-between

Markup: grid/row-space-distribution.html
